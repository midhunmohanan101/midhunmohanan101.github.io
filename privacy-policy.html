Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PDF Utility – Merge & Compress ("the App") handles user information. Your privacy is important, and this App is designed to operate without collecting or sharing personal data.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal or sensitive user data. There is no account creation, login, or user tracking functionality.

2. File Processing

All PDF operations, including merging and compression, are performed entirely on the user's device. Your files are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on external servers.

3. Internet Usage

The App does not require an active internet connection to function. No data is transmitted over the network during normal operation.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, including analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

5. Permissions

The App uses the Android Storage Access Framework (SAF) to allow users to select files for processing. Access is limited only to files explicitly chosen by the user.

6. Data Security

Since all operations are performed locally on the device, user data remains under the user's control at all times. No external storage or transmission of data occurs.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or any users.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the App or regulatory requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
